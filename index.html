Historical Finishes

Other Notable Mentions:

  1. MacGregor vs. Alvarez - McGregor becomes the first simultaneous double champion in UFC history with one of the most dominant title fight performances
  2. Oliveira vs. Gaethje - Oliveira ties Nurmagomedov's record for title defenses in lightweight after being knocked down twice in a round and then submitting Gaethje
  3. Pereira vs. Ankalaev 2 - Pereira avenges his loss in the rematch with a knockout in less than 2 minutes, reclaiming the UFC Light Heavyweight title
  4. Ngannou vs. Overeem - One of the most brutal knockouts in heavyweight history
  5. Masvidal vs. Askren - The fastest knockout in UFC history (5 seconds)
  6. Rousey vs. Correia - Ronda Rousey's dominance in her prime years
